FUEL PLAN:    ***DOES NOT ACCOUNT FOR WIND***    Fuel Plans include:

Minimum: 
Minimum takeoff fuel requied to destination + 1hr reserve.  Legs 2-8 taxi fuel is included in the minimum.   

Alternate:
Planned fuel burn from Destination airport to Alternate airport specified in cell C21.

Extra:
Additional fuel above the minimum amount, selectable between +05 to +45 minutes, the default setting is +05. 
Dispatch and Pilots plan minimum then add fuel for weather, icing, known contingencies, IFR congestion, holding, etc. as required to have an adequate feel supply.  Top Mains and TOP ALL selections are available. 
 
Takeoff:
Fuel onboard when beginning the takeoff roll (START fuel minus 1st Leg TAXI fuel).  Used for W&B calculation.

Taxi 1st Leg:
AFM maximum taxi fuel to meet Max Ramp Weight limitation when loaded to the structural MGTOW. 

START:
The total amount on the guages after refueling, before engine start.  Verify this amount (GOM 11.02) and sync the fuel totalizer (except the C212, reset the fuel totalizer to zero).

Legs 2-8 Takeoff fuel equals previous leg Remain fuel minus 4 minutes taxi fuel. 
[10]
Flt BO = Flight (enroute) Burn Off.

Planned inflight fuel burn off. 

1st leg Taxi fuel is accounted for prior to takeoff. 

Subsequent legs taxi fuel is accounted for at 4 minutes per leg.  It is displayed as the difference in "Remain" and next leg "Takeoff", and is included in the "Minimum" calculation and total plan burn.
[11]
Remain equals Takeoff - Flt BO. 

Note that the next leg Takeoff Fuel equals the Remain value minus 4 minutes of taxi fuel allowance.

Distance Notes:

***Pilots must verify distance with other sources.  Errors may exist.  Notify benr@beringair.com for corrections. 

All distances calculated to show realistc mileage by:
1. Haversine Great Circle formula,  
2. Rounded up to nearest 1nm, and
3. Added 3nm for terminal area distance.

Some routes have special routing rules to give more accurate actual distance, as follows: 
1. All flights between SVA/GAM/OHC and mainland north of 63N and ANC are routed via shorter of CUMNS or ZONBA. 
2. All flights between SVA/GAM/OHC and mainland south of 63N other than ANC are direct.  Liferaft/vests may be required. 
3. All flights between PHO/LUR and OME are routed via SUGRE. 
4. All flights between NW and SW Alaska are routed via ..TOCOT-POYIT.. for single engine glide distance.  Multi-engine may have shorter routes available while remaining within 50mn of shore, or extended overwater more direct with liferaft/vests onboard. 
5. All flights to DRG from north of DRG route via JIKSA, except LUR, PHO, KVL are direct.  
6. All flights between UNK and airports northwest of 64N/162.5W routed via AHCUJ-THEPS.

***these routes are not required to be flown, rather used to add reasonable mileage for basic calculations***

***this program is for basic planning purposes only, check against other planning tools available***

***WIND NOT ACCOUNTED FOR***

***For long flights, especially one way or high altitude, ForeFlight Performance Pro (BE20) will be more accurate.  For other aircraft, the basic Foreflight will still be more accurate when wind and cruise altitude are included***

*** Verify distances, as they were gathered using AI.  Email benr@beringair.com for corrections, additions, or information***
