Flight Into Known of Forecast Icing (FIKI) conditions is determined by:

1. Reference to appropriate Icing Forecast, and

2. Reference to company or official PIREP's within < 3 hours along route/altitude of flight.

3. Check the FIKI box if light or greater icing is indicated (uncheck if conditions allow).

4. C208: reduce MGTOW to arrive in icing area < 8807 lbs.

    All aircraft adhere to AFM icing limitations.


FIKI ADDITIONAL 

DISPATCH / OPERATIONAL CONTROL

1. Check the FIKI Box if light or greater icing is indicated (Uncheck if conditions allow)

PILOT

1. Check the FIKI Box if light or greater icing is indicated (Uncheck if conditions allow)

2. Plan ice mitigation strategy (route, altitude, step-climb, fuel burn prior to icing)

3. Plan icing exit strategy (routes, altitudes, alternates, freezing level, bases/tops)

4. Enter a short FIKI Remark with the reason and enter first leg MGTOW in Flight Report.

5. Further legs MGTOW set at pilot's discretion.

C208

The C208 requires a takeoff weight between 8807-9062 so that the aircraft will arrive in the icing environment at or below 8807 lbs. Each leg MGTOW is at pilot discretion based on the conditions for that leg. Fill TKS tank to an appropriate level at or above 11.7 gallons for FIKI conditions. Remain clear of icing or embedded forecast icing until gross weight is less than 8807 lbs. If icing is encountered over 8807 lbs, EXIT icing immediately.
